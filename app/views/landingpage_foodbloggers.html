Our Love Story

Like hundreds and thousands of food bloggers, I use Instagram to grow my blog’s presence. It’s an amazing place to share content and is playing a bigger role in how people discover recipes. As an active user of Instagram myself, I’d like pictures of food daily but when the time came to cook these recipes I could never remember whose feed the post was on and the recipe would be lost.

I realised if this was happening to me, it was also happening to my followers and the millions of followers of food bloggers all over the world. As a result, followers miss out on great recipes and food bloggers lose traffic. I wanted to solve this problem and so Love To Eat It was born.

Sahar Aman - Founder & CEO
